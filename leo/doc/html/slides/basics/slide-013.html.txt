Undo and redo
=============


Leo has unlimited undo/redo capabilities.

Here, I typed Ctrl-Z to undo the previous move.
The "go shopping" node is once again a child of
the "to do" node.

**Note**: Ctrl-Shift-Z redoes a previous undo.
