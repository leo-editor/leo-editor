Using the minibuffer quickly becomes second nature. When in doubt about what the valid completions are, you simply hit the <tab> key.

Here are a few more details you should know about:

  • You can use <BackSpace> in the minibuffer to show more alternatives.
  • You can hit <Ctrl-G> at any time in Leo to put focus in the body pane. This is the way to exit the minibuffer without executing any command.
